
## Business Objective

To analyze supermarket sales data in order to:
- Identify top-buying customers
- Find best-selling and least-selling products and categories
- Understand branch-wise and city-wise performance
- Analyze customer behavior by gender and customer type
- Study time-based sales trends (day, month, weekday, year)
- Support data-driven business decisions



## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional sales data including:
- Product category
- Quantity sold
- Revenue and profit
- Customer type and gender
- Payment method
- City and branch
- Date and time of purchase



## Key Analysis & Insights

### Category-wise Sales Analysis
- **Fashion Accessories** is the top-selling category.
- Indicates strong customer demand and frequent purchases.



### Customer-Type & Gender Analysis
- **Normal and Membership customers** contribute almost equally.
- Sales are **evenly distributed across male and female customers**.


### City-wise Sales Contribution
Top contributing cities:
- **Yangon**
- **Mandalay**
- **Naypyitaw**



### Revenue vs Quantity Analysis
- Strong **positive linear relationship** between quantity sold and revenue.
- High-volume products significantly increase total revenue.



### Payment Method Analysis
- Customers mostly prefer **E-Wallet and Cash**.
- **Credit card usage is relatively low**.


### Top-Selling Products (Revenue)
- **Food & Beverages**
- **Sports & Travel**



### Least-Selling Products
- **Health & Beauty** shows the lowest performance in both revenue and quantity.



### Top-Selling Products (Quantity)
- **Electronic Accessories** record the highest quantity sold.
- Indicates strong recurring demand.



### Month-wise Sales & Profit
- **February** shows a noticeable drop in profit.
- **January and March** maintain steady profit levels.



### Day-wise Sales Insights
- Highest sales observed on **15th and 19th** of the month.



### Weekday-wise Sales Analysis
- Top-performing days:
  - **Saturday**
  - **Tuesday**
  - **Wednesday**



### Branch-wise Revenue Analysis
- **Branch 3** generates the highest revenue.
- Reflects strong customer engagement and sales performance.



## Business Recommendations

### Product & Category Strategy
- Introduce bundle offers (e.g., Fashion + Accessories, Charger + Earphones)
- Launch seasonal and festive promotions
- Promote products via social media influencers
- Upselling and cross-selling using premium versions
- Flash sales to drive impulse buying
- Sampling campaigns for low-performing categories
- Warranty extensions for high-demand electronics



### City & Branch Improvements
- Increase local promotions in high-performing cities
- Optimize inventory based on city-wise demand
- Introduce city-exclusive discounts



### Time-Based Sales Optimization
- Schedule product launches on **15th and 19th**
- Run 1-day exclusive offers on peak dates
- Introduce weekday special promotions
- Increase ad spending on profitable weekdays


### Customer Engagement & Marketing
- Personalized marketing based on customer segments
- Loyalty programs with reward points
- Exclusive offers for membership customers
